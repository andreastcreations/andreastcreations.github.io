Celestial Solids

  Celestial Solids is a virtual instrument for Android created with Unity3D and C#. It was one of my first projects where I was experimenting with Wwise (audio middleware software) and MIDI.

  My plan was to create a simple musical instrument that would sound well enougn on mobile phones' speakers and that would also have dynamics to give it a more realistic sound.

You can download the app from the Google Play Store for free.
