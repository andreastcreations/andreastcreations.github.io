Mushrooms

  Mushrooms is a spawner game made with C# and Unity3D. You are in control of a colourful mushroom that shoots spores on enemy waves. There are 5 waves and a boss, and after that it repeats indefinitely. With each cycle, enemies' life goes up by a percentage and they give you more points. Considering the fact that there are no consumables that fill your health bar, it is getting harder and harder pretty fast. When you lose all your lives, game resets.
  All 3D models and animations were made in Blender, sound effects and music were made in Reaper.

You can download the app from itch.io for free.
